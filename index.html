What the Crêpe?

MARVIN, currently in Beta, is a self-learning voice interface that will help you find the perfect restaurant through conversation.

You can chat with MARVIN in a natural and human way about all kinds of things you are looking for in a restaurant - this can range from the type of food you fancy, the price range you are happy with or even if it has Wi-Fi availability.

You can also ask MARVIN to call the restaurant, open their website, ask about special requirements (such as a kids or a Vegan menu) and even give you directions using your devices' own navigation system.

  • Speak Naturally

    No keywords, special phrases or speaking like the Queen. MARVIN likes your voice just how it is.

  • Any style of food

    Whether you fancy a curry, a calzone or a crab shack; MARVIN will know exactly what you mean.

  • Ask Questions

    Does it have a Vegan menu, what about Wi-Fi? Do I need to dress smart? MARVIN knows.

  • Make it so

    Once you've found a place, you can ask to call it, open it's details and navigate to it. All by voice.

  • Tap and type

    If you're in a library and cannot speak, you can tap an option or type a response too.

Frequently asked questions

What can I talk to MARVIN about?MARVIN knows all about restaurants. Discuss any aspect you are interested in. If you are not sure what to ask for just say “help”.

Does MARVIN understand Scottish?Yes, MARVIN understands many different variations of English.

Will MARVIN come to iOS?Yes, MARVIN will - signup to our mailing list above to find out as soon as its available on the Apple Store.

How many restaurants can MARVIN talk about?At present MARVIN can talk about around 200,000 different UK restaurants and this is always increasing.

Can I ask for a restaurant directly by name?Yes, simply ask for the restaurant by its name or spell it out to MARVIN.

Can I use my own navigation software for directions?MARVIN will always open the default applications for directions, viewing websites and making phone calls.
